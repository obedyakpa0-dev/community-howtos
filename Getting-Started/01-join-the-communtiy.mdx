# Join the Codetopia Community

Welcome! We're glad you're here. The Codetopia Community is a space for
builders, learners, and creators figuring it out together. Before you can
access anything, you need a portal account. Every member of the Codetopia
Community has an identity — and yours starts here.

## Create your portal account

Head over to [portal.community.codetopia.org](https://portal.community.codetopia.org)
and create your account. The portal will walk you through everything you
need to get set up.

A few things to keep in mind:

- Use your **real, accurate personal information**. Every member of the
  Codetopia Community is expected to represent themselves honestly.
- Members found using information that does not belong to them will have
  their accounts removed or banned without notice.
- Take your time with the onboarding — read everything carefully and fill
  in all required details correctly.

## What's next?

Once your portal account is set up, you're ready to join the community
server and link your account.
